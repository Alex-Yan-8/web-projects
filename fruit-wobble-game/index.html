The Goal

Fill the score bar to the target before you run out of moves. Do it by merging and popping fruit. Here's how 👇

🍎🍎🍎

1 · Drag to Merge

Drag across 3 or more fruit of the SAME color. They combine into one bigger fruit where your drag ends. (Each drag is one move.)

👆💥

2 · Tap to Pop

Tap a merged (bigger) fruit to pop it for points. The bigger the fruit, the way more it scores — so merge big before you pop!

⚡🔥

3 · Build HYPE

Every pop fills your HYPE multiplier (top of screen). Keep popping to ramp it up to ×5 — it multiplies ALL your points. It drains if you stop, so keep the combo rolling!

🍊✨

4 · Fire Powers

Popping also charges the power icons above the jar. When one glows, tap it for a big effect (grow fruit, bombs, score, and more).
